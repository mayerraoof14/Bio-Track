Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the readings?
Our device provides medical-grade accuracy with ±1% margin for heart rate, ±2% for blood oxygen, and ±0.1°C for temperature measurements.
What is the battery life of the device?
The device has a battery life of up to 7 days on a single charge, depending on usage.
Can I sync the device with my smartphone?
Yes, the device can be synced with our mobile app available for both iOS and Android.
Is the device waterproof?
Yes, the device is water-resistant and can be worn while swimming or showering.
